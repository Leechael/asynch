Interpretation
--------------

The local reference source build is 3.5% to 14.1% below the installed Cython
wheel on this runner. This is a source-build versus wheel measurement, not an
isolation of Cython compiler effects: both paths execute C extensions, and
compiler/toolchain differences remain possible.

The asynch-to-wheel delta is modest for Int64 and LowCardinality on this host,
but is substantial for String (5.4% to 5.5% of wheel throughput) and Nullable
(13.3% to 14.1%). This is a single-query decoding comparison only. It does not
measure Pool contention, and it does not demonstrate protocol-level concurrency
or multiplexing.
